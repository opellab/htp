We present a dynamic ontology for the structured classification of mental health dimensions within the HiTOP model.

This ontology is designed to flexibly respond to new research findings and integrate future expansions into the taxonomy.

It encompasses a wide range of categories, from broad Superspectra to specific manifestations like symptoms and disorders, offering a detailed hierarchy of mental health attributes.

Through continuous adaptation and expansion, our ontology enables precise and up-to-date representation of psychological phenomena.
